Klaviyo Email Sequences
5 Sequences — Built & Ready

Klaviyo is installed on your site. These sequences are not active externally. We write them, build them in Klaviyo, and hand off for approval. You own everything regardless.

Sequence 1 — Virtual Assessment Follow-Up

Triggered when someone submits the online assessment. 3 emails over 7 days. Goal: convert the soft inquiry into a booked consultation. Tone: warm, informative, low pressure.

Sequence 2 — Pre-Consultation

2 emails between booking and appointment. Sets expectations, reduces no-shows, primes the patient on the i360 process. Increases show rate and first-visit satisfaction.

Sequence 3 — Post-Appointment

3 emails after the first visit: thank you + review ask (timed to peak satisfaction) → maintenance schedule introduction → re-booking soft prompt with educational framing.

Sequence 4 — 90-Day Reactivation

Triggered when a patient hasn't booked in 90 days. One email based on last treatment type. Pulls back lapsed patients with a relevant, non-promotional reason to re-engage.

Sequence 5 — Monthly Value Send

One educational email per month. Not promotional. Draws from your RealSelf Q&A library. Positions Dr. Mabrie as the authority, not just the provider. The long-term retention engine.
